1. The lion is a species in the family Felidae.
2. It is a muscular, deep-chested cat with a short, rounded head, a reduced neck and round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
3. Living in the grasslands, scrub, and open woodlands of sub-Saharan Africa, the lion is the second largest cat in the world (behind tigers).
4. African lions are the most social of all big cats and live together in groups or “prides.” ...
5. Lions live for about 10-14 years in the wild.
