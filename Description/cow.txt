1. Cow, in common parlance, a domestic bovine, regardless of sex and age, usually of the species Bos taurus.
2. Domestic cows are one of the most common farm animals around the world
3. A cow has 32 teeth. A cow will chew about 50 times in a minute, making their jaws move about 40,000 times a day.
4. The main stomach of a cow, the rumen, holds up to 50 gallons of food that has been partially digested. Referred to as cud
5. A cow will chew for up to eight hours a day.
